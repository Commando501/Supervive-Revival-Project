THE FULL ROOT-CAUSE CHAIN (all now understood)
----------------------------------------------
1. N=0 "Invalid replicated field 0" — the FIELD-INDEX space diverged because
   SUPERVIVE's AActor has 11 CPF_Net props (stock 10). FIXED by injecting
   ServerState at RepIndex 10 (step 2 v1).
2. "Invalid property terminator handle - Handle=22" — the finer REPLAYOUT
   HANDLE space still diverged. Diagnosed via LogRepProperties VeryVerbose on
   the stub: our stub emitted a MAX handle of 22, the client's layout tops out
   at 21 — a clean off-by-one. Root cause: our ServerState mirror was a plain
   member-wise USTRUCT (2 cmds: State + Version), but SUPERVIVE's
   PoolableActorServerState is a SINGLE RepLayout cmd (custom NetSerialize, like
   stock FRepMovement). Every handle after ServerState was shifted +1.
   (Note: an intermediate attempt to fix State's enum bit-width — plain int32
   32 bits vs the client enum's CeilLogTwo(4)=2 bits — did NOT change Handle=22,
   which is what proved the issue was structural/count, not bit-width.)
3. FIX: gave FPoolableActorServerState a NetSerialize + TStructOpsTypeTraits
   WithNetSerializer=true. RepLayout then collapses it to ONE cmd. Verified live
   via LogRepProperties: our stub's max handle dropped 22 -> 21, matching the
   client. PC bunch accepted.
